Jubilee Los Lunas

Los Lunas, New Mexico

Jubilee Los Lunas is New Mexico's premier Active Adult community. Nestled at the foot of El Cerro, this community is just 20 minutes from downtown Albuquerque and the airport. Anticipate all the exciting new choices at Jubilee, including the brand new swimming pool, community garden, observatory, tennis courts, pickle ball courts and shading for our bocce ball courts. Live life to the fullest, from sunrise to sunset, day after day at Jubilee. Enjoy wonderful new friends, a beautiful home and magnificent Villa, amazing scenery and more choices than you've ever experienced before. It's all here for you at Jubilee. Enjoy the many activites that bring you together with your friends. Zumba, Yoga, book club, backyard barbecues, wine and cheese parties, morning coffee, bike rides, dog walks, cooking classes, stained glass classes, jewlry classes, movie nights, drinks on the patio, day trips and more.
